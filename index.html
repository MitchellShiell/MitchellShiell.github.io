About

Hey! My names Mitchell Shiell, I'm a trained scientist, dedicated educator and passionate creative. I love teaching yet currently cannot afford the cost of teachers college. However, teachers teach so i hope to use this website to do just that. I created this space originally as my first coding project but hope to expand it as my experimental laboratory/showcase for educational content. My current goals for this site are as follows:

  • 1. Provide multimedia content covering the entirety of grade 11 and 12 Biology (based off the Ontario Curriculum).
  • 2. Produce educational content for entertainment purposes on various topics in relation to the sciences.
  • 3. Develop a unique and recognizable online identity and aesthetic

Thanks for visiting, I hope you find value here! If you have any suggestions feel free to contact me by email: Mitchell.shiell@gmail.com
